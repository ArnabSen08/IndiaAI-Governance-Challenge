IndiaAI, under the Ministry of Electronics and IT (MeitY), has partnered with the Real Time Governance Society and the Government of Andhra Pradesh to launch an innovation challenge that invites developers, startups, and organizations to build AI-driven solutions addressing critical governance challenges.
